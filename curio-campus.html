Curio Campus

Peer-to-Peer Collaboration & Emergency Support Platform

Curio Campus is a productivity and collaboration app built to help students and professionals connect, assist each other, and work together in real time. The platform enables users to create skill-based help requests and receive instant support from peers through chat, calls, or group communication.

The Goal

Curio Campus is designed to make peer assistance faster, organized, and skill-oriented â€” combining communication, coordination, and teamwork into one smart platform. It aims to bridge the gap between people who need help and those who have the skills to provide it, all within a localized or community-based context.

Key Features

  • Emergency Skill-Based Help: Users can create urgent help posts based on specific skill requirements for immediate response.
  • One-on-One & Group Comms: Supports video/voice calls, private chat, and group chat for seamless collaboration.
  • Project & Task Management: Track projects, create subtasks, assign responsibilities, and organize by skills.
  • Skill Tagging & Discovery: List your skills and find the right experts for any task or collaboration.
  • Dark Mode Support: Fully integrated dark theme for a comfortable and focused user experience.
  • FCM Push Notifications: Real-time alerts for messages, calls, help requests, and task updates.
