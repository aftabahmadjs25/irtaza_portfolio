PC Genius

Smart PC Recommendation & Custom Build Estimator

PC Genius is a comprehensive two-module application designed to simplify the PC building process for everyone. Whether you're a complete beginner or a seasoned tech enthusiast, the app provides the tools needed to plan, cost, and finalize your ideal computer build.

The Challenge

Building a PC can be an intimidating task due to the sheer volume of components and compatibility requirements. For non-technical users, understanding the relationship between budget, usage (gaming vs. office), and specific hardware is a major hurdle. Even for tech-savvy users, manually tracking prices and managing configurations across multiple components can be tedious and prone to calculation errors.

The Solution

PC Genius bridges this gap with a dual-module architecture. It serves as both an automated consultant and a granular management tool.

For beginners, the Smart Recommendation Engine takes care of the complexity. For experts, the Custom Estimator provides full control. By centralizing component selection and cost calculation into one intuitive interface, the app makes PC planning faster, more accurate, and accessible to all.

Dual Module Capabilities

  • Guided Recommendations: Enter budget and usage (Gaming, Office, Editing) to get a complete, compatible build instantly.
  • Custom Build Estimator: Manually pick CPU, GPU, RAM, and Storage to see a real-time total cost breakdown.
  • Smart Compatibility: The app ensures that the recommended parts work together seamlessly within your budget.
  • User-Centric Design: Simple, intuitive navigation designed to make technical data easy to digest.

Technical Implementation

The app was engineered using Flutter to provide a fluid, responsive experience across mobile platforms. The recommendation logic utilizes a structured database of component tiers and pricing to ensure accurate results.

The Impact

PC Genius empowers users by removing the fear of the unknown in hardware selection. It transforms a complex, multi-hour research project into a few simple taps. For enthusiasts, it serves as a digital scratchpad that eliminates manual math, while for beginners, it acts as a trusted advisor, ensuring they get the best value for their money.
