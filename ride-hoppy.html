Ride Hoppy

Free Corporate Ride Sharing Platform

Ride Hoppy is a two-sided mobile application solution designed to connect passengers with corporate employees who are willing to offer free rides during their daily commute. The platform promotes community-based transport, reduces commuting costs, and encourages corporate social responsibility.

Key Features

Driver App (Corporate Employees)

  • Create profile & verify identity
  • Set travel route, schedule & availability
  • Accept or reject ride requests

Rider App (Passengers)

  • Request free ride by location
  • View availabile corporate drivers
  • Track ride status & communicate

Note: Although rides are free, passengers have the option to tip the driver based on their experience.
