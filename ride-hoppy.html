Ride Hoppy

Free Corporate Ride Sharing Platform

Ride Hoppy is a two-sided mobile application solution designed to connect passengers with corporate employees who are willing to offer free rides during their daily commute. The platform promotes community-based transport, reduces commuting costs, and encourages corporate social responsibility.

The Challenge

For many corporate employees, the daily commute is a significant source of stress and expense. Public transport can be unreliable or crowded, while ride-hailing services like Uber or Careem become prohibitively expensive for daily travel. Furthermore, large organizations often suffer from "silos," where employees rarely interact with colleagues outside their immediate teams. There was a need for a solution that could solve the mobility problem while simultaneously building a stronger corporate community.

The Solution

Ride Hoppy bridges this gap by creating a trusted, internal ride-sharing network. Unlike commercial platforms, Ride Hoppy is exclusive to corporate employees, ensuring a high level of safety and trust. The app operates on a "freemium" community model where rides are free, fostering a culture of helping one another.

The system consists of two distinct interfaces: the Driver App for those with vehicles, and the Rider App for passengers seeking a lift. By matching routes and schedules, we optimize empty seats and reduce the total number of vehicles on the road.

Core Experience

For Drivers (Providers)

  • Route & Schedule: Easily set daily commute routes and available timings.
  • Request Management: View rider profiles and accept/reject requests based on convenience.
  • Verification: Corporate email verification ensures only legitimate colleagues can join.

For Riders (Passengers)

  • Smart Matching: Find drivers passing near your location at the right time.
  • Live Tracking: Real-time map tracking to know exactly when your ride arrives.
  • Safety First: View driver ratings and verified profiles before booking.

Project Impact

By facilitating carpooling, Ride Hoppy directly impacts the organization's carbon footprint, supports green initiatives, and significantly lowers commuting costs for junior employees. Beyond the metrics, it serves as a powerful networking tool, allowing employees from different departments—who might never meet otherwise—to connect during their ride.

Note: Although rides are free to encourage usage, passengers have the option to tip the driver as a gesture of appreciation.
